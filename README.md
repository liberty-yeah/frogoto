# Recursive Minimalist Notepad

### How it works
With this you can **Nest** another identical notepad within a single word. And do so infinitely!

### The Buttons
* **🗺️ Map:** Shows a tree view of all your notes so you can see how they are connected.
* **🌱 Sprout:** Highlight a word and click this to turn it into a sub-note.
* **❌ Delete:** Turns on "Delete Mode." Click a blue note to remove it. Click ❌ again to turn it off.
* **⏳/💎 Save:** Click the hourglass to turn on auto-save. The diamond means your work is safe in the browser.
* **B / I:** Makes text **Bold** or *Italic*.
* **田 Grid:** Shows background lines like a paper notebook.
* **W Wrap:** Keeps long sentences from running off the edge of the screen.
* **# Lines:** Shows line numbers on the left side.
* **💾 Export:** Saves your entire project as a file on your computer.
* **📁 Import:** Opens a project file you saved earlier.
* **✖ Reset:** Wipes everything. It clears all notes and starts fresh.

---

### Example
`Travel to Japan [Flights] [Hotel] [Sightseeing]`
Instead of having addresses, flight numbers, and ticket prices all on one screen, you **collapse** that info. You click **[Hotel]** to see the address. When you are done, you click "Root" at the top to go back to the main list. 

### Navigation
* **Going In:** Click any blue word in brackets to open that note.
* **Going Out:** Use the "Breadcrumbs" at the top (Root » Note Name) to move back to the previous page.
